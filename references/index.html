Methodology Notes

Research Approach: This compilation draws from peer-reviewed academic literature, government reports, case studies, and industry publications published between 2009-2022. Priority was given to recent studies (2018-2022) and those with direct relevance to urban food access, pharmacy deserts, and hub-spoke distribution models.

Geographic Focus: While examples span urban, rural, and international contexts, emphasis was placed on models applicable to urban environments similar to Seattle's demographics and regulatory environment.
